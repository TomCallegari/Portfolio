Humidity



Latitude and Humidity show an interesting relationship; as you move closer to the equator humidity readings have less deviation and congregate above 60. As you move away from the equator humidity readings have greater deviation with humidity levels moving from approximately zero to 100 at or around 25 degrees of absolute latitude.
